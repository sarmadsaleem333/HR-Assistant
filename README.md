A web-based HR assistant that helps recruiters **shortlist, rank, and evaluate candidate CVs** using configurable and transparent quality criteria.

---

## Features

- Upload CVs in **PDF/DOCX** format (OCR supported for scanned files)
- Automatic extraction of:
  - Education
  - Work Experience
  - Publications
  - Awards & Achievements
- **Configurable scoring and ranking** using YAML/JSON weights
- Transparent score breakdown per candidate
- Evidence-backed explanations and candidate comparisons
- Privacy-aware processing with optional PII redaction

---

## How It Works

1. Upload candidate CVs
2. System parses and structures CV content
3. HR-defined weights are applied for scoring
4. Candidates are ranked by final score
5. Results and explanations can be exported

---

## Outputs

- Ranked list of candidates
- Per-CV scores and sub-scores
- Explanation of why one candidate ranks higher than another
- Exportable JSON/CSV reports
